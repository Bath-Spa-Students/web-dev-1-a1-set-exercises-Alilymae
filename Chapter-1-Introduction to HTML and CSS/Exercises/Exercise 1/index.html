Yakisoba (Japanese Stir-Fried Noodles)

What is Yakisoba?

Yakisoba is a classic Japanese stir-fried noodle dish that’s seasoned
with a sweet and savory sauce similar to Worcestershire sauce. Use any
proteins you like—pork, chicken, shrimp, or calamari. For vegetarians,
just swap it with tofu or shiitake mushrooms.


Cooking Tips



  1. Use a large cookware.


    It’s important to use a griddle, wok, or frying pan with a large cooking space so that your ingredients have direct
    contact with the hot surface. The nice char will add fantastic flavors and give the noodles a “street food” character.

  2. Cook vegetables in stages.


    When it comes to any stir-fried dishes, we want to retain the fresh texture and crunch of the vegetables. So it is
    important to stir-fry the hard vegetables first before adding other softer ingredients. Each vegetable has a different
    cooking time, so cook quickly and in succession. Do not throw everything at once. Also, do not overcook as wilted
    veggies are a no-no for stir-fries.

  3. Don’t add too many ingredients.


    This is after all a noodle dish. It’s tempting to toss in lots of veggies or extra meat. Adding too many ingredients
    will end up steaming the noodles. You need to let the steam escape, so the noodles are not wet, and preferably make
    it as dry and crispy as possible. If you prefer drier-style noodles (I prefer mine to have some moisture), fry the
    noodles first, then transfer them out before cooking the vegetables and meat/ tofu. That way, you don’t have to cook
    the noodles later on, and the noodles will still stay crispy.

Ingridients


  • Yakisoba Noodles
  • Green Onion
  • Sliced garlic
  • Capsicum (Red Bell Pepper)
  • Chopped Cabbage
  • Mushrooms
  • Onion
  • Chicken
  • Vegetable oil
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Oyster sauce
  • Ketchup
  • Soy sauce
  • Sugar

Cooking Method


  1. Cut the ingredients into bite-size pieces.

  2. Boil the noodles for 3 mins then run under cold water to stop from cooking further.

  3. Add vegetable oil in a pan and fry the chicken till its golden brown, season with salt and pepper and set aside.

  4. In the same pan fry the white part of the green onion and garlic till the aroma sets then add the chopped
    cabbaged carrots and fry till they're soft. Set aside along with the shrimps

  5. Mix all fried ingridients and noodles and season them with Yakisoba Sauce. Add the sauces and adjust as
    suited to your preferences

  6. Top the noodles with chopped green onions then serve.
